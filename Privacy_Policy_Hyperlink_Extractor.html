🔐 Privacy Policy for Hyperlink Extractor Extension

Effective Date: [17/05/2025]

1. 📌 Information

We do not collect or process any personal or sensitive information.

The Extension performs the following local actions:

  • Scans the currently open web page in your browser
  • Extracts and displays hyperlinks and associated text present on that page

All actions are executed locally within your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored by the Extension.

2. 🔄 Data Usage

The Extension only temporarily accesses page content to extract hyperlinks for display purposes. This data:

  • Is not logged
  • Is not stored
  • Is not transmitted outside your device

No cookies, trackers, analytics, or server calls are involved.

3. 🚫 Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. The Extension does not use any third-party services, scripts, or APIs that collect user data.

4. ✅ Compliance with Google Policies

This Extension complies with:

  • Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy
  • Google API Services User Data Policy (if applicable)
  • GDPR and other international data privacy regulations

5. 🔐 Security

As there is no data collection, there is no risk of unauthorized access or data breaches related to user information. We implement best practices to ensure code-level safety and user privacy.

6. ❌ No Personal or PHI Data

The Extension does not refer to, collect, or store:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
  • Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • Sensitive personal data such as financial or biometric information

7. 🗑️ User Control

You may uninstall or disable the Extension at any time through your browser’s Extension settings. No data remains on your device after uninstallation.
